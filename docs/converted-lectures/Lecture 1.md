1. Introduction to the industry analysis
1. Definition
• An industry represents a collection of enterprises that produce products
using similar technologies, rely on uniform (similar) resources, and operate
within the boundaries of an industry-specific market.
• In sectoral analysis, the object of study is the industry, which is one of the
most important meso-economic structures.
